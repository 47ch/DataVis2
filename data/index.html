Internet Usage Across the World

As technology progresses, people all around the world become further exposed to the benefits of being constantly connected to the Internet. This visualisation serves to showcase how quickly people across the world gained access to the internet.

Individual Countries' Adoption of Broadband

In some countries, being able to access the internet is the least of their worries. Countries where most of the citizens have access to food, water and shelter have a higher percentage of their population owning a broadband subscription.

Difference Between Each Country's Approach

The number of internet users for each country depends largely on their population. Philippines in this case have an incredibly steep gradient upwards as compared to their population growth.

Mobile Connectivity for Everyone!

Many people in today's world have multiple phones, mainly to assign a separate purpose to each phone, for example, a work phone and a personal phone.

Share of Internet Users
